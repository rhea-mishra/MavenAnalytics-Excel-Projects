# Maven Excel Projects

## Pr0ject 1 - Data Analyst at Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)

#### SITUATION
You work as a data analyst at Los Angeles International Airport (LAX), the largest and busiest airport on the West Coast of the United States and the eighth busiest in the world

### BRIEF
You have access to the airport’s flight delay and cancellation data*, which includes information on each flight’s airline, destination, and departure and arrival times
</br> </br> Your goal is to leverage your Excel skills to manipulate and analyze the data so your manager can share the airport’s performance to the Federal Aviation Administration

### OBJECTIVE
**Use Excel to:**
</br> • Create calculated columns from existing data
</br> • Join data from multiple related tables
</br> • Summarize the data to gain meaningful insights </br>

## Project 2 - Sales Manager at Maven Electronics

### SITUATION
You work as a Sales Manager at Maven Electronics, an electronics retailer that sells computer components to businesses across the world

### BRIEF
You have access to the company’s CRM data, which has information on all the sales opportunities, including the sales agent, product, account, and deal stage. 
</br> </br> Your goal is to leverage your Excel skills to analyze trends and patterns in the sales pipeline and identify insights to improve your team’s performance.

### OBJECTIVE
**Use Excel to:**
</br> • Explore & analyze the data
</br> • Create new calculated fields
</br> • Find insights to improve your team </br>
